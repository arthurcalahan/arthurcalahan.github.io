## ğŸ“… What is Deferred Apps?

Deferred Apps provides a smart way to access applications on your system. Instead of downloading every app you might use, it creates shortcuts that download the actual packages only when you launch them for the first time. This approach saves space and keeps your launcher tidy. Perfect for rarely used applications you want to keep handy.
